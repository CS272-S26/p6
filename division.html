Division

Division is splitting a total number of items into equal groups to get the quotient.

Division can be very helpful in everyday life. For example, lets say you have two bunnies named Hopper and Pogo. These two get together with a recipe book and settle their eyes on an apple pie that calls for 4 apples. Excitedly, they hop back to their gardens and find that they have a total of 24 apples.

How many apple pies do you think Hooper and Pogo can make with the apples that they have?
