Welcome to Simple Math Learning Tool!

Simple Math Learning Tool is a website designed for young users (like you!) to learn some elementary-level math. We try our best to keep it entertaining and fulfilling as possible, so that you don't have to stress about it in class.

Here, you can learn how to add, subtract, multiply, and divide all while having fun! Go through our interactive pages and soon you'll be a math master! You can either refer to the navigation bar at the top of this page or continue reading and you'll figure out where to go!

Want to learn addition?

If you're new to math, addition is the best one to learn first. We'll give you a basic rundown so you can comfortably learn how to add at your own pace.

Let's learn subtraction!

Already know how to add but don't know how to subtract yet? Then click below and it'll take you to the perfect page!

How about multiplication?

Learning multiplication will take you to another level of math masterty if you already know addition and subtraction. Show us what you got!

Ready to challenge yourself with division?

Division is the ultimate challenge to math. Master division and you'll for sure be the best mathematician in town!
