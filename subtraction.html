Subtraction

Subtraction is the process of taking one group of things away from another group to find out how many are left, called the difference.

Subtraction can be very helpful in everyday life. For example, lets say you have two bunnies named hopper and pogo. These two get together with a recipe book and settle their eyes on a carrot cake that calls for 10 carrots. Excitedly, they each hop over to their respective gardens to see what they can find. Pogo comes back with 7 carrots and hopper came back with 4 carrots. Their ears droop with disappointment as they realize 4 is not enough and neither is 7. Then, Pogo has the great idea to combine their carrots into one big group and see if they have enough!

How many carrots do you think Pogo and Hopper have together?
